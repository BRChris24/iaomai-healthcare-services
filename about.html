Our Story

IHS is a professional home healthcare agency providing compassionate, high-quality care in the comfort of your home. We are built on a simple belief, that healing should not remain in the hospital, but rather begin or continue from home where people feel safest and most supported. Our services is delivered by licensed professionals with professionalism, empathy, and respect for dignity. At Iaomai, we go beyond clinical care. We listen, we support, and we walk alongside individuals and families through every stage of their healing journey , because every home and every person matters.

OUR CORE VALUES

  • Compassion

    We care deeply about the people we serve. Every interaction is guided by empathy, kindness, and respect for the individual behind the condition.

  • Professional Excellence

    We uphold the highest standards of clinical practice. Our services are delivered by trained, qualified, and licensed professionals committed to continuous improvement.

  • Dignity & Respect

    We honour the dignity of every client, recognising their autonomy, privacy, culture, and personal values within their own homes.

  • Trust & Integrity

    We understand that inviting healthcare providers into one’s home requires trust. We act with honesty, accountability, and transparency at all times.

  • Person-Centred Care

    We tailor our services to meet the unique needs of each client and family, ensuring care is personalised, flexible, and responsive.

  • Collaboration

    We work closely with families, caregivers, and healthcare partners to ensure continuity of care and the best possible outcomes.
